This is my Chemotaxis project, I called it Artistic Amoebas. The little amoebas follow your cursor is a random way, always trying to move towards the cursor but with some random movement. You can click to disperse them away from the cursor. This is one of my favorites because the patterns it makes have cool layers and watching the amoebas recollect after they are dispersed is very satisfying.
